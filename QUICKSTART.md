# Tripatti - Quick Start Guide

## For Users

### Getting Started
1. **Sign up** with email/password or social login
2. **Create or join a trip**:
   - To create: Enter trip name, pick emoji & currency
   - To join: Enter the 6-character trip code shared by a friend

### Creating a Split (Expense)
1. Go to trip Dashboard
2. Tap **"Add Expense"** button (top right of Members or in Expenses section)
3. Enter amount and description
4. Choose who paid (default: you)
5. Choose who's included (default: all members)
6. Tap **"Add Expense 💰"**
7. Everyone gets a notification

### Paying a Split
1. In trip Dashboard → **Expenses** → tap **"Details"** on any expense
2. Find your split in the expansion
3. Tap **"Mark Paid"** when you send the money
4. Payer will see a notification and can tap **"Confirm"** to confirm receipt
5. Once both confirm, split shows "Settled ✓"

### If You Created an Expense
- **Edit**: Tap "Edit" on the expense card to change details or members
- **Delete**: Tap trash icon to remove (can't undo!)
- **Remind**: In the expansion, tap the 🔔 **"Remind"** button next to unpaid splits to nudge members

### Checking Balance
- **Members panel** shows:
  - Red "Pay X" = money you owe
  - Green "Get X" = money owed to you
  - Amber badges = payments pending confirmation

### Settling All Debts
1. Go to **"Settle"** tab
2. See all minimum transfers needed
3. Make payments outside app (Venmo, bank, cash)
4. Mark as paid when you pay, confirm when you receive
5. Celebrate when it says "All Settled Up! 🎉"

## For Trip Creators

### Managing Your Trip
- **Share code**: Tap share icon at top to copy invite code
- **Add members**: Share code with friends; they join via "Join Trip"
- **Create splits**: Anyone can create, but you can edit/delete/remind
- **Manage expenses**: Only you can edit/delete your expenses

### Best Practices
1. ✅ Create splits immediately after paying
2. ✅ Use "Remind" before asking people to pay
3. ✅ Confirm payments once received
4. ✅ Check settlement after everyone pays
5. ✅ Keep trip for records until everyone settles

## Troubleshooting

### "Payment Failed"
→ Check internet connection, try again

### "I can't see X person's payment"
→ Realtime updates take a few seconds; refresh the app

### "Edit/Delete button missing"
→ You must be the person who created that expense

### "Payments showing wrong amount"
→ Exact match required; make sure amount matches the split exactly

### "Push notifications not working"
→ Allowed notifications in browser/phone settings
→ Try refreshing the app or logging out/in

## Tips & Tricks

💡 **Categories**: Use them to see what you spent on (Food, Transport, etc.)

💡 **Currency**: Set at trip creation; all amounts in that currency

💡 **Offline**: App works offline; changes sync when back online

💡 **Sounds**: Tap sounds toggle in settings for feedback on actions

💡 **Dark mode**: Follows your phone settings

## What's Being Tracked?

**Your trip data**:
- All expenses and who paid
- All splits and who owes what
- Payment confirmations
- Your push notification subscription

**We don't track**:
- What you spent the money on (description is just for you)
- Your location or device
- How many times you use the app

All data encrypted in transit and at rest.
